# Deal Similarity System - Complete Documentation

## Executive Summary

The **Deal Similarity System** is an intelligent AI-powered platform designed to help private equity and investment firms quickly identify similar historical deals when evaluating new investment opportunities. By combining structured financial data with unstructured document analysis (CIMs, investment memos), the system enables analysts to find relevant comparable deals in seconds, dramatically reducing research time and improving decision quality.

---

## 1. Business Problem & Solution

### The Challenge

**For Business Users:**
- Investment analysts spend 20-30% of their time manually searching for comparable deals
- Critical historical context is buried in CRM systems and document repositories
- Manual comparisons are subjective and time-consuming
- New analysts lack institutional knowledge of past deals
- Finding the right comparables impacts investment decisions and deal pricing

**Current Process:**
1. Analyst receives new CIM (Confidential Information Memorandum)
2. Manually searches CRM system for similar companies
3. Reads through historical documents and memos
4. Compiles comparison spreadsheet
5. **Time Investment: 4-8 hours per deal**

### Our Solution

**Automated Similarity Search:**
1. Analyst uploads new deal information
2. System automatically finds similar historical deals in **seconds**
3. Presents ranked results with similarity explanations
4. **Time Savings: 4-7 hours per deal**
